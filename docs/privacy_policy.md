# Privacy Policy for Luxr

Last updated: [DATE]

## Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Luxr:

**Account Information**
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile photo (optional)

**Order Information**
- Shipping address
- Order history
- Purchase amounts

**Device Information**
- FCM token (for push notifications)
- Device type and OS version

## How We Use Your Information

- To process and fulfill your orders
- To send order status notifications
- To improve app performance
- To provide customer support

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase,
protected by industry-standard encryption.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.
We share data only with:
- Firebase (Google) for app infrastructure
- Payment processors for order processing

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your data via Profile settings
- Delete your account and all data
- Opt out of promotional notifications
- Request data export
